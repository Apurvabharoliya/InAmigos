Our Story

InAmigos Foundation was founded on September 23, 2020, by Mr. Govind Shukla with a vision to create meaningful impact through education, awareness campaigns, sustainability drives, women empowerment initiatives, and community support activities. We believe in building a compassionate future where individuals work together to uplift communities and inspire social transformation. Through collective action, volunteering, and awareness, we are shaping stronger communities and helping individuals become changemakers for society.

Section 8 Registered

Common Questions

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

InAmigos Foundation is a registered Section 8 NGO dedicated to raising the standard of living, environmental protection, youth skill development, women empowerment, and animal welfare across India. We believe that small individual acts of compassion combine to create massive ripples of positive social transformation.

Becoming a volunteer is simple! You can click any of the "Join the Movement" or "Volunteer" buttons across our website to launch our interactive onboarding form, or register directly with our volunteer team. We offer flexible options, including remote online roles and on-field direct community outreach drives.

Yes, we are a fully recognized and registered Section 8 non-profit organization under the Central Government of India. We are backed by active 12A & 80G tax certifications for transparent operation, registered under CSR-1 to collaborate on Corporate Social Responsibility projects, and aligned with NITI Aayog objectives.

100% of community contributions go directly toward running our grassroots projects. Your funds purchase school supplies for BachpanShala, provide critical veterinary care and nourishment for Project Jeev, purchase sewing machines and vocational training tools for Project Udaan, and buy saplings and plantation equipment for Project Prakriti.
